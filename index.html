How we met

We met in church, not thinking much of it at first — just two people in the same place, at the same time. But somehow, little moments kept bringing us closer. Looking back now, it’s hard not to see it… God was quietly placing us in each other’s path all along.

Events

Day One — Saturday, July 25th

Vow Ceremony 7:00 AM

Kidane-Mehret Eritrean Orthodox Tewahedo Church
5140 129 Ave NW, Edmonton, AB T5A 4N3, Canada

We'd love for you to join us as we begin our day in church. This is the most meaningful part of our wedding for us — a traditional Eritrean Orthodox ceremony where we receive our vows and blessings. It's a beautiful and sacred moment, and having you there to witness it would mean a lot.

Wedding Ceremony & Reception 6:00 PM

Srpska Sala – Edmonton Serbian Cultural Centre
12920 122 St NW, Edmonton, AB T5E 6J1, Canada

In the evening, come celebrate with us. This is where we get to relax, enjoy, and be fully present with everyone we love. Expect great food, music, and a full night of dancing — we really just want to share this joy with you.

Day Two — Sunday, July 26th

Hamawti 9:00 AM

The following day, we'll be continuing the celebration with Hamawti — a more intimate, traditional gathering with close family and loved ones.

This portion of the celebration is by invitation, and we'll be reaching out directly with details.

Dress Code

Vow Ceremony — Formal & Modest 7:00 AM

For the church, we kindly ask guests to dress respectfully in line with the Eritrean Orthodox tradition. Think formal attire with modest coverage — longer dresses, covered shoulders, and classic, elegant looks. This is a sacred setting, and we want everyone to feel comfortable and appropriate for the ceremony.

Reception — Formal / Elegant 6:00 PM

In the evening, we invite you to dress up and celebrate with us. Formal attire is encouraged — suits, dresses, and elegant evening wear. This is a night to look your best, enjoy, and dance the night away.
